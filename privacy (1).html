Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 10 October 2025

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

DigitalABCs ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988. Our mission is to be transparent and empower you, and that extends to how we handle your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that is necessary for us to provide our services to you. This may include:

  • Personal Identification Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and business name when you fill out our contact form or engage our services.
  • Project Information: Details and documents related to your business that you provide for us to deliver our AI consultancy services. This information is treated as strictly confidential.
  • Payment Information: When you purchase a service, your payment details are processed securely by our payment processor, Stripe. We do not store your full credit card information on our servers.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-identifiable information about how you interact with our website (e.g., pages visited, time spent on site) to help us improve our user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Manage Our Services: To create your client account, deliver the services you purchase, and communicate with you about your project.
  • To Process Payments: To securely process transactions for our services.
  • To Communicate With You: To respond to your inquiries, send you proposals, invoices, and important updates about our services.
  • To Improve Our Business: To understand how our clients use our services and website so we can improve our offerings and customer experience.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business. These include:

  • Google Workspace: For email communication and file storage (Google Drive).
  • Tally: For our website contact forms.
  • Make.com: To automate our internal workflows.
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing.
  • Softr: For our client portal.
  • Vapi AI & Twilio: For automated client check-in calls.
  • DocuSign: For electronically signing contracts and proposals.

We ensure these partners are compliant with strict privacy and security standards.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We use a combination of technical, administrative, and physical controls to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Our client data is stored securely within Google Drive with restricted access.

6. Your Rights

Under Australian law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct your personal information: If you believe any information we hold is inaccurate or out of date, please contact us to have it corrected.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Cookies

Our website may use 'cookies' to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files placed on your computer's hard drive. They help us analyse website traffic and improve our site. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please get in touch with us.
