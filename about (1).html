Our Story Starts with a Real Struggle

DigitalABCs wasn’t born in a boardroom. It was born from the real-world struggles of our founder, Trish, a single mum of two. After COVID, she faced the daunting challenge of re-entering a workforce that had been transformed by automation. She saw firsthand how technology could feel like a barrier—a tool for corporate giants, not for everyday people.

She experienced the frustration of workplace micromanagement and the inefficiency of systems that were meant to help but only made things more complicated. She knew there had to be a better way.

Instead of being pushed out, Trish pushed back. She dedicated herself to learning AI and automation, not to climb a corporate ladder, but to build a ladder for others. She discovered that AI wasn't something to be feared; it was a tool that could give power back to employees, communities, and small businesses.

DigitalABCs is the result of that journey.
